Karnataka

Explore Karnataka

Karnataka, a state in southern India. offers a rich tapestry of history, culture, and natural beauty: Known for its diverse landscapes and vibrant traditions, Karnataka is an ideal destination for travellers seeking a unique blend of experiences. Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, a culinary enthusiast, Karnataka has something to captivate your interest. Karnataka, with its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes, is a destination that promises a memorable experience. From the ancient ruins of Hampi to the royal splendour of Mysore, the serene beaches of Gokarna to the lush hills of Coorg, Karnataka offers something for every traveller. Don't forget to savour the state's delectable cuisine and explore its bustling markets

NORTH KARNATAKA

A total 4 days trip planned for you which includes.....
Visiting the ruins of HAMPI for a day and heading towards GADAG which is 108.9 km away which takes 2.5 hr to reach. It includes visiting historical places, temples, statue and a zoo. Then the next place is HUBBALII which is famous for its beautiful handloom industry which is 59.5km away and takes 1.10 hr. BADAMI which is 104.8km away and takes nearly 2.20 hr is famous for its rock cut monuments such as the Badami cave temples, as well as the structural temples such as the Bhutanatha temples, Badami Shivalaya and Jambulingesvara Temple. PATTADAKAL ,a town in the Bagalkot district of Karnataka, is known for its ancient architecture and many temples is 21.9 km away and takes 30 min to reach . AIHOLE  has historical significance and has been called a cradle of Hindu rock architecture which is 13.3 km and takes 20 min . ALMATTI DAM including a park and rock garden which is 41.7km away and takes 1.20 hr. Then our last stop is BIJAPUR also known as VIJAYAPURA  famous for its historical monuments and architectural marvels which is 70.1km away and takes 1.20 hr .
